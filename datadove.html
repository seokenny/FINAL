Data Dove

Data Dove is focused on securing the most coveted and hype products available on the market.

What is Data Dove? Data Dove is a premium private Discord group dedicated to ensuring members purchase upcoming hyped products as soon as they are released and available.

Creating a brand

As a new group, Data Dove had no logo or website and was a clean slate. I was given the task to design a logo as well as a website. The only requirement for the logo was that it must include a bird or dove.

I worked on the logo design first before moving onto the website. From there, I would use the features of the logo throughout the website.

Logo Design

The idea of an origami bird was the first thing that came to mind. I felt the straight lines gave off a very tech-like feel, which matched the theme of Data Dove. I went for a dark main color with a bright pink to complement it.

Website Design + Develop

For the website, I used the same color as the logo but added complementary colors to bring more life and personality. The background was kept dark to give off a private, secret group while using it to make the main colors more vibrant.
